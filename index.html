Multi-Chambered Profile Anatomy

Our uPVC systems are manufactured using state-of-the-art multi-chamber profiles equipped with heavy-duty galvanized steel reinforcements inside. This architectural design ensures ultimate security, high loading strength, and superior thermal performance.

  • EPDM Rubber Gaskets: Double sealing prevents atmospheric dust, heavy monsoon rain, and sound waves from passing through sashes.
  • Argon-Filled Double Glazing: A 12mm cavity filled with dry Argon gas acts as an absolute barrier against summer heat waves and traffic noises.
  • Fusion-Welded Corners: All frame joint corners are thermoplastically fusion-welded to form a monolithic, leak-proof structural frame.
  • Multi-Point Locking: High-grade hardware secure sashes at multiple slots simultaneously, providing top-tier residential security.
